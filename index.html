Instructions

Swipe to right

Objective

Guess the hidden 4-digit number in 4 tries.

Rules

A number in the correct spot will be highlighted in green.

A number in the wrong position will be highlighted yellow.

A number not in the solution will be grayed out.

Notes

Empty Input will be taken as 0

There are no duplicates in the hidden number
